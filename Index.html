Queer South Asians (QSA) is a Toronto-based, Trans-led non-profit organization dedicated to creating a safe, inclusive, and supportive community for LGBTQIA+ South Asians. Our mission is to empower individuals by fostering connections, celebrating cultural heritage, and providing resources for personal growth, self-expression, and advocacy. All of our programs and events prioritize Transgender and queer south asian emerging artists, performers, youth, newcomers and immigrants.
