Artspace: Roots Revived is a multidisciplinary art based skill building program for LGBTQ+ youth ages 16-29 who are part of South Asian communities in Toronto.

This program offers three cohorts - Visual arts, Dance and Drag, facilitated by experienced local artists from the queer South Asian community, and will provide exciting opportunities for youth to creatively explore their connection to their cultural roots, queer and trans identities and art based storytelling.

Queer South Asians
Queer South Asians (QSA) is a trans-led and volunteer-run community group that organizes culturally affirming events and support programs for LGBTQ+ South Asian community in Toronto.

If you would like to know more about us please find us on IG or email!
