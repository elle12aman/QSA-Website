About QSA
Queer South Asians (QSA) is a trans-led and volunteer-run community group that organizes culturally affirming events and support programs for LGBTQ+ South Asian community in Toronto. QSA was founded by Arshi Syed on April 2nd, 2015.
QSA Mission Statement
Our mission is to center the lived experiences of Queer South Asians by fostering a vibrant space for cultural expression, peer support, and collective care.

Through community-led art practices, storytelling, and intentional gatherings, we aim to build deep connections, nurture resilience, and honor the richness of our identities.

QSA Vision
We envision a world where Queer South Asians can live boldly at the intersection of all their identities—where queerness and culture are not in conflict, but in conversation.

A world where ancestral wisdom meets radical love, where community care is a daily practice, and where we gather in joy, resistance, and healing. In this space, we are not just surviving—we are creating, connecting, and thriving together.
